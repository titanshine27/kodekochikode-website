A3K Rajagiri Hackathon

A3K Rajagiri Hackathon is a 24hr hackathon to be conducted in Rajagiri School of Engineering & Technology on March 14-15. Prizes worth 1.5Lakh to be won

Buy Ticket

About the event

The hackathon will kick off at 9AM on March 14th and go on till 9AM in the followind day, March 15th. The conclusion of the coding sessions will be followed by the kickstart of the evaluation processes. The coding session will also involve mentors from the industry who will be providing their valuable experience and knowledge to the participants
